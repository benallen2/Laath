About Me


Laath Martin graduated from the University of North Carolina at Greensboro with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Design. He currently works at Jackson Library at UNCG as Lead Designer and Digital Media Specialist in the Digital Media Commons. He is a photographer, graphic designer, musician, and filmmaker at heart, and uses these varying methods to craft his work. His photography background is quite documentary, with projects chronicling the Moral Monday rallies in North Carolina and others abroad in Ghana, Germany, France, and Spain. His passion is progress, intimacy, and conversation. Through the experiential truth of photography, he starts this interaction with his subject and viewer. He has no favorite color or hobby, no lucky number, and no true sense of a hometown. Instead, he tries to enjoy it all.
